ASTEROIDS DEFENCE

Its been over ten thousand years since you and other brave heros have been defending the Earth from imminent destruction. Many have perished while attempting to shield humanity from its inevitable fate. Generations of space defenders have fallen, and you shall go down in history as the Earth's last space defender.

Aware that a single collision with an asteroid would explode your ship, you look around for your fellow space defenders. You notice that all the remaining space defenders have been destroyed by asteroids. The only one left is you. You are planet Earth's only chance for survival.

Your mission is to defend the Earth from its inevitable destruction for as long as possible. The Earth has shifted into a new and more dense asteroid belt and is constantly in the path of danger. Humanity is unaware of its fate. Keep them alive for the longest possible time at all costs.

How To Play

  • Press arrow keys to move up/down/left/right
  • Press R to rotate right
  • Press L to rotate left
  • Press spacebar to shoot laser
  • Press P to pause game
